Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files a website saves on your device through your browser. They can remember your preferences and choices so your experience runs more smoothly.

What This Site Stores

AstroPhonia uses only essential technical storage. It is strictly necessary for the site to work and cannot be switched off:

  • ap_lang — remembers the language you chose (EN / IT / PT).
  • ap_cookies — records that you have seen this cookie notice.

Both values are kept in localStorage, not in traditional cookies. They never leave your device and are never transmitted to any server. They have no expiry date and are removed when you clear your browser data.

Because this storage is strictly necessary to provide the service you requested, no prior consent is required under Art. 122 of the Italian Privacy Code and the ePrivacy Directive. The notice you see is informational.

What We Do NOT Use

  • Advertising or tracking cookies
  • Analytics tools (Google Analytics or similar)
  • Social media pixels or trackers
  • Profiling of any kind
  • External fonts, icon libraries or scripts loaded from third-party servers

All resources needed by this site — fonts, icons, images, scripts — are served from this same domain, so browsing these pages sends nothing to third parties.

Third-Party Platforms

When you follow a link to Spotify, YouTube, Amazon Music, Bandcamp, Instagram or WhatsApp, you leave this site and their own cookie and privacy policies apply. We have no control over the cookies those platforms set.

How to Control This Storage

You can delete or block localStorage data through your browser settings at any time. Doing so simply resets your language choice and the cookie notice. Private or incognito windows do not retain this data at all.
