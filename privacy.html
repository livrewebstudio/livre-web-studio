2. What Data We Collect

This website does not collect, process or store personal data about its visitors. There are no contact forms, no user accounts, no newsletter, no analytics and no advertising or profiling tools.

All fonts, icons, images and scripts are served from this same website. No content is loaded from external providers, so simply browsing these pages does not send your data to any third party.

3. Local Storage

Two values are saved in your browser's localStorage so the site works as expected:

  • ap_lang — the language you selected.
  • ap_cookies — the record that you have seen the cookie notice.

These are technical values only. They stay on your device, are never sent to any server, and you can delete them at any time from your browser settings. Full details in our Cookie Policy.

4. Hosting and Server Logs

Like any website, this one is delivered by a hosting provider whose servers may record technical access data — including IP addresses — for security and operational purposes. This processing is carried out by the provider under its own privacy terms, and is necessary for the website to be reachable at all (legitimate interest, Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

5. Third-Party Platforms

This site links out to external services: Spotify, YouTube, Amazon Music, Bandcamp, Instagram and WhatsApp. These links only open when you click them. From that moment you are on those platforms, and their own privacy policies apply. AstroPhonia does not receive, process or store any data you share there, including payment data.

6. Your Rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port and object to the processing of your personal data. Since this website does not collect personal data, in practice there is nothing to access or erase — but if you believe otherwise, write to contato@astrophonia.com and we will reply within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority — in Italy, the Garante per la protezione dei dati personali (garanteprivacy.it).
